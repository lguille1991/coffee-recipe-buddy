# 1Zpresso Q-Air Grind Table

The Q-Air uses **rotations from 0** (fully closed). It is a lighter, travel-friendly grinder.

**Scale:** 0.0 (finest) to ~4.0 (coarsest), measured in full rotations from the zero position.
**Resolution:** ~350 microns per full rotation (approximate, non-linear).

---

## Notes

- Report Q-Air settings to one decimal place (e.g., 2.4, not 2).
- Range should span ~0.3–0.5 rotations.
- Zero point: fully close the grinder until burrs meet, then open to your target rotation.
