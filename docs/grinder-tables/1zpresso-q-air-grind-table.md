# 1Zpresso Q-Air Grind Table

The Q-Air uses a **R.C.M** (rotations.clicks.micro-adjustments) setting notation.

- **Rotations**: full 360° turns from the zero position (fully closed)
- **Clicks**: 10 clicks per rotation
- **Micro-adjustments**: 3 micro-adjustments per click (30 per rotation)

Example: `2.5.1` = 2 full rotations + 5 clicks + 1 micro-adjustment

**Scale:** `0.0.0` (finest) to ~`4.0.0` (coarsest).
**Resolution:** ~350 microns per full rotation (approximate, non-linear).

## Notes

- Report Q-Air settings in R.C.M format (e.g., `2.4.0`, not `2.4`).
- Range should span ~3 clicks (0.3 rotations).
- Zero point: fully close the grinder until burrs meet, then open to your target setting.
- Each micro-adjustment is the finest step; 3 micro-adjustments = 1 click, 10 clicks = 1 full rotation.
